About


You can call me Zeesun (pronunciation of my Chinese name) or Herman (yes, I picked it by myself when I was in elementary school).

With experiences in full spectrum of technology industry, I believe that internet and software will continue to revolutionize our daily life. My variety of professional and academic experiences also made me realize that my true passion lies in utilizing my analytical and programming skills to create products and applications that will benefit people’s lives.


I am constantly looking for new challenges and willing to relocate to any exciting metropolis, preferably the bay area, Washington DC, or Dallas.
