Summary

Stats Portal is a web application I built for managers to monitor live information including tickets summary, client temperature, and team member's performance.

This is an extremely valuable experience for me. During the process, I advanced my understading of software life cycle. I created this website from scratch solely by myself (from UI design, MVC architecture, documentation to implemention, refactoring, testing, deployment).

After the success of initial release, I received more requests from different teams. As a result, three other of my colleagues joined me to continue improving the site. I started to lead scrum meetings and organize disucssions with my teammates. I also became to focuse more on ensuring the quality of the code
